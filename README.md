# AURAA: Emotion-Aware AI Companion 🌌🤖

AURAA is a sophisticated, emotionally intelligent digital companion that combines real-time computer vision, large language models (LLMs), and interactive 3D avatars to provide a personalized, empathetic user experience.

## 🚀 Overview
The primary objective of AURAA is to bridge the gap between human emotion and machine interaction. By "seeing" you through your webcam and "sensing" your emotional state, the AI transcends basic chatbots to become a true digital companion.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js 16, Three.js (Fiber), Framer Motion, Tailwind CSS.
- **Backend**: FastAPI, Socket.IO, Groq (Llama-3), OpenCV/FER.
- **Database**: Firebase Firestore (Auth & Persistence).

## 📱 Page Breakdown
- **Login/Signup**: Glassmorphic entry with 3D parallax effects.
- **Selection**: Choose between the "Bot Core" (Holographic) or "AI NextGen" (Humanoid) interfaces.
- **Companion Stage**: Main dashboard with real-time emotion tracking, empathetic chat, and smart music/movie recommendations based on your mood.

## ✨ Key Features
- **Neural Gaze Tracking**: Avatars follow your mouse cursor in real-time.
- **Persistent Memory**: Secure, encrypted history of all your conversations.
- **Mood Recommendations**: Dynamic Spotify and Movie suggestions tailored to your detected emotion.
